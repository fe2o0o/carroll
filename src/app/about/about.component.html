About Us

Car Roll Dice Offers six membership types to meet the unique needs of our buyers.

  • 3D Models

    Simply Making three-dimensional representations of cars so that our customer can see the detail of the car besides the real picture of it. It helps online bidders to watch the car as if it’s physical right their sight.

  • Free spectating: Normal membership and VIP membership: In Free Spectating; Our website visitors can see cars whether it’s a normal picture or a 3D modeling, without bidding.

    In Normal Membership; Our website customers who want to buy less than 10 cars in a year, in this feature they can see multiple auctions and bid up to 20,000 EGP without a deposit, also they can bid up to 4 cars in the same time but with deposit.

    In VIP membership: Our VIP customers who want to buy more than 10 cars in a year can have all normal membership feature plus the upcoming features; they can see multiple cars and bid up to 120,000 EGP with a deposit. Receive priority customer service and phone support.

  • Special offers

    Maintenance offers depends on membership subscription.

  • Test Driving

    In the announced date, only people who subscribed in the online auction, they can test the cars they willing to bid on.

  • Accident Car Auction:

    Accident vehicles listed in a car roll dice auction include details about the extent and nature of the damage they sustained. This may include information on the type of accident, visible damage to the exterior or interior, whether or not the vehicle is in operation, and any specific repairs needed. We provide photos showing the damage, allowing potential buyers to evaluate the condition before bidding and purchasing the vehicle.
