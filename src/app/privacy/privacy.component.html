    • Refund Policy: If the primary winner declines the purchase, their deposit is refunded, and the process moves to the secondary winner.

FAQs for Car Roll-Dice

 

General Questions

 

Q1: What is Car Roll-Dice?

A1: Car Roll-Dice is an online auction platform specifically designed for buying and selling cars in Egypt. It offers a dynamic and competitive environment where buyers (bidders) can place bids on vehicles listed by sellers (auctioneers).

 

Q2: How does Car Roll-Dice work?

A2: Sellers list their cars on the platform, providing detailed information and images. Buyers place bids on the cars they are interested in. The highest bid at the end of the auction period wins, and the winning bidder can inspect and test drive the car before completing the purchase.

- Basic Member: Enhanced features including increased bids and listing capabilities for 300 EGP per month.

- Premium Member: Full access with unlimited bids and listings, premium support, and VIP features for 800 EGP per month.

A4: You can upgrade or cancel your membership through your account settings on the platform. Memberships are billed monthly, and you can cancel at any time to stop future payments.

 

 

 

Bidding and Buying

 

Q5: How do I place a bid on a car?

A5: After registering and logging in, you can browse car listings and place bids on any car that interests you. Simply enter your bid amount and confirm it to participate in the auction.

 

Q6: What happens if I win an auction?

A6: If you win an auction, you will be notified and given the opportunity to inspect and test drive the car. If you are satisfied, you can proceed with the purchase. If not, the next highest bidder will have the opportunity.

 

Q7: What is the process if I am one of the multiple winning bidders?

A7: If the highest bidder declines to purchase the car after inspection, the next highest bidders (secondary winners) will be invited to inspect and purchase the car in order of their bids.

Q8: How do I list a car for auction?

A8: As a registered user, you can list cars by providing detailed information, including photos, videos, and a description of the car. You can list up to 2 cars per month for free, with increased limits for Basic and Premium members.

 

Q9: What fees are associated with listing a car?

A9: Each car listing incurs a fee of 500 EGP. This applies to cars in categories such as used cars, customized cars, and accident cars.

 

Q10: How does the agency contract work to prevent broker scamming?
